Quick Pay

Instantly make your payment using a variety of available facilities, such as bKash, Nagad, MasterCard, Visa card, and other payment methods.



Pay Bill through bKash

Login

Login to your bKash account with your account's PIN number and tap on "পে বিল" option.

Select Dot Internet

In "পে বিল" option you'll find "Dot Internet". Simply tap on it and follow the next step.

Select Billing Timeline

Now select your internet billing timeline in "বিলের সময়সীমা" box & input your customer in "বিল অ্যাকাউন্ট নম্বর দিন" box.

Save Payment Info

If you want to save the payment info for quick payment, you can simply tick the checkbox "ভবিষ্যৎ বিল পেমেন্টের জন্য একাউন্টটি সেভ করে রাখুন".

Complete Payment

After that, tap the "পে বিল করতে এগিয়ে যান" button & hold the pay button for a few seconds. You're done!
